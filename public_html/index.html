E-ASL What can we offer?

First started as an attempt to bring new life to ASL (American sign language) in terms of reaching a wider audience E-ASL was started in an attempt to open up ASL to all users for free. E-ASL aims to provide a service in which a multitude of different users can access a host of different learning materials to learn ASL, which has traditionally been a language that has been taught face to face.

  • 01 What kind of learning content is available? S

    E-ASL offers high quality video tutorials that cater both to the hearing and deaf communities. these in depth lessons will teach users how to perform basic signs but do grow harder in difficulty as the user progresses.

  • 02 What are the quizzes?

    Quizzes are fun way to test the user after each video lesson has been completed. once a lesson has been completed the user will be prompted to take the quiz to check that the information that was learnt in the lesson has been retained and thus to see if they are ready to proceed to the following lesson.

  • 03 Why go choose our service?

    E-ASL endeavours to provide a top quality product while maintaining this learning platform for free to all users for life. E-ASL will routinely add further updates and content throughout the lifecycle of this project.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does this work?, isnt sign language better taught face to face?

    Up until recently sign language was a very hard subject to teach online due to its viusal nature which meant face to face tuition was a necessity but with technology advancing to the point it currently has this fails to be a hurdle any further as technology is now at a point that allows for conference calls and visual media to be utilised competently.

  • How much is this going to cost me?

    Absolutely free, here we believe that sign language is not a service but a right.

  • where do I start?

    Jump in now at lesson 1 in the lesson hub and after each lesson take the quiz to see if your ready to progress to the next lesson.

  • What is the 3D visualiser?

    Sometimes showing the exact shape a hand makes to create a sign can be difficult to convey so with the assistance of 3d web rendering users can view a sign in 360 degrees to full grasp how a sign is to be made.
