Lesson 3

Lesson 3 Manners and further small talk.

In this lesson some basic manners are taught using ASL so that they can be used when appropriate in conversation. other elements of conversational small talk are also taught in this lesson.Small talk lets you find out a little about someone you’ve just met, and it’s as prevalent in Sign as it is in the spoken word. This video shows two people having a little chat at a dinner party using American Sign Language (ASL).
